The Problem





To measure the scientific literacy/temper amongst crosssection of population



An app based questionnaire / feedback form with the use of only graphics/charts/pictures without text material could be developed for analysing feedback.

Solution Architecture


  1. 1. Scientific temper is a way of life - an individual and social process of thinking and acting - which uses a scientific method, which may include questioning, observing physical reality, testing, hypothesizing, analyzing and communicating.
  2. 2. Describes an attitude which involves the application of logic. Discussion, argument and analysis are vital parts of scientific temper.


“Scientific temper may be defined as a temperament which connotes a type of frame or disposition of mind which is free from superstition, prejudice, rigidness, obscurantism, close-mindedness, irrationality, un-innovativeness, subjectivity and other parochial tendencies.”
